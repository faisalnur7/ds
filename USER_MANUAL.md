# Darus Salam CCIMS User Manual

## Login

1. Open the app at `/`.
2. Sign in with your assigned account.
3. Admin users are redirected to the admin dashboard.
4. Members are redirected to the member dashboard.

## Member Workflow

### Dashboard

- Review your share count, approved payments, documents, and checkout eligibility.
- Use the quick actions to jump to payment history, checkout requests, or profile settings.

### Payment History

- Open `Payments` from the navigation.
- Review each payment month, approval status, amount paid, and payment method.

### Checkout Requests

- Open `Checkout Requests` from the navigation.
- Choose `Full` or `Partial`.
- If partial, enter the percentage you want to request.
- Add any outstanding loan deduction if applicable.
- Submit the request and wait for admin review.

### Profile

- Update your account name, email, and password from the profile page.

## Admin Workflow

### Members

- Create and manage member records from the admin area.
- Keep KYC and member identity data up to date.

### Payments

- Create payment records for member share contributions.
- Use the receipt page to print or download a CSV copy of the receipt.
- Enable `Auto approve payments` in Settings if payments should be approved automatically.

### Checkout Requests

- Review member requests from the admin checkout request module.
- Update request status to approved, rejected, or paid as needed.

### Reports

- Open the Reports page to review ledger totals.
- Use `Export CSV` to download transaction data for the selected date range.

## Settings

- `Auto approve payments`: approve eligible payments automatically.
- `Email notifications`: enable or disable email delivery.
- `Notification channels`: master switch for system notifications.
- `Checkout eligibility window`: number of months before a member can request checkout.

## Notes

- The member dashboard and checkout pages are data-driven and reflect live records.
- Receipt downloads are CSV-based, and receipt pages remain printable.
- If a member cannot access checkout requests, confirm their member record exists and their join date satisfies the eligibility window.
